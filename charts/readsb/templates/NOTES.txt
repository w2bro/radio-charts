It is important to blacklist the kernal modules on the nodes so that the host's kernal doesn't take over the device.

Please run the following commands on the host:

blacklist rtl2832
blacklist dvb_usb_rtl28xxu
blacklist rtl2832_sdr

For more information, visit mikenye's setup guide https://github.com/mikenye/docker-readsb/wiki/Guide-to-ADS-B-Data-Receiving,-Decoding-and-Sharing,-Leveraging-RTLSDR-and-Docker
